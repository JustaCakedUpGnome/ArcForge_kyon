🎯 TRAINING REVOLUTION 🎯

Mentzer fundamentally changed how people think about training:

Before Mentzer:

  • "More is better" mentality
  • Daily training sessions
  • High volume, low intensity
  • Following champions blindly

After Mentzer:

  • Quality over quantity focus
  • Recovery-based programming
  • High intensity, low volume
  • Scientific approach to training

🌟 LASTING INFLUENCE 🌟

Modern training concepts rooted in Mentzer's work:

  • High-Intensity Training (HIT)
  • Periodization and Recovery
  • Evidence-Based Training
  • Individualized Programming
  • Mind-Muscle Connection
  • Training to Failure
