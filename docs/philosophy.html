Mike Mentzer applied Ayn Rand's Objectivist philosophy to bodybuilding, emphasizing:

  • Reason over Emotion: Training decisions based on logic, not feelings
  • Individualism: Each person must find their optimal training approach
  • Rational Egoism: Pursue your own excellence without compromise
  • Objective Reality: Facts exist independent of beliefs or traditions
"Man's mind is his basic tool of survival. To live, he must think."

"Logic is the art of non-contradictory identification."
