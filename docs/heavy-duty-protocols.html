‚ö° INTENSITY PRINCIPLE ‚ö°

Training to Momentary Muscular Failure

  • Each set performed to complete muscular failure
  • Rep range: 8-12 for most exercises
  • Slow, controlled movement (4-2-4 tempo)
  • No momentum or cheating
ONE SET TO FAILURE > MULTIPLE HALF-SETS

üî• WORKOUT STRUCTURE üî•

Brief & Intense Sessions

  • Maximum 45 minutes per session
  • 6-9 exercises total
  • 1 working set per exercise (after warm-up)
  • 2-3 minute rest between exercises
QUALITY OVER QUANTITY

Infrequent Training Schedule

  • Minimum 48-72 hours between sessions
  • Training frequency: Every 4-7 days
  • Listen to recovery indicators
  • Growth occurs during rest, not training
