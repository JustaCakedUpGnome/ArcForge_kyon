Sample Workout Routine

Date: June 17, 2025

Duration: 45-60 minutes

Focus: Upper body strength

Warm-up (10 minutes)

  • 5 minutes light cardio (treadmill/bike)
  • Dynamic stretching
  • Arm circles and shoulder rolls

Main Workout

1. Bench Press

4 sets × 8-10 reps

Weight: 135 lbs

Notes: Focus on controlled movement, 2-second pause at chest

2. Incline Dumbbell Press

3 sets × 10-12 reps

Weight: 30 lbs each hand

Notes: 30-degree incline, full range of motion

3. Pull-ups/Lat Pulldowns

3 sets × 6-8 reps

Weight: Bodyweight / 120 lbs assistance

Notes: Wide grip, chest to bar

4. Overhead Press

3 sets × 8-10 reps

Weight: 95 lbs

Notes: Standing, core engaged

Cool-down (10 minutes)

  • Static stretching
  • Chest and shoulder stretches
  • Deep breathing

Post-Workout Notes

Felt strong today. Bench press form was solid. Need to work on pull-up strength - assisted reps felt challenging but manageable.

Next session: Focus on legs and glutes
